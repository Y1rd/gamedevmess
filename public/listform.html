Term Definition Use Picture
Aerodynamics The study of the properties of moving air and the interaction between the air and solid bodies moving through it. To understand interaction, allowing for proper aircraft.
Aerostat An airship or hot-air balloon, especially one that is tethered. Mainly used as a thermostat.
Asteroid A small rocky body orbiting the sun, being a object that is neither a true planet nor a comet. Tends to be studied as to why they exist, also to prevent any from coming to Earth.
Freefall A downward movement under the force of gravity only. Jumping off any extremely high hight sends you into this state, skydiving for example.
